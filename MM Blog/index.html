About

James and Michael are making happiness in the great state of Colorado. Whether its a weekend adventure to the mountain, a home cooked meal for friends, or a project to just make home feel a little bit more like home. This is our lives and our stories. Hope you enjoy what you read and it inspires you to live your own best life.

James is a CPA working for a Tech Start Up in Denver, CO. He recently purchased his first starter home in Denver and is eager to share his home improvement stories with you. He is a wandering soul who moved to Colorado from Florida. He is still getting use to the winters, but believes a sunny day should never be wasted, learn his favorite Denver spots for the perfect day hike.
Michael is a librian south of Denver. He spent 6 months learning culinary skills in the West of France. Let him tell you the story of food and culture. Also don't forget his long time friend, a lab, Adison who will no doubt find her way into all of posts.
